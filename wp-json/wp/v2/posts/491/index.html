Who are you and what do you create?<\/strong><\/p>\n

My name is Evelyn Marina and I create abstract artworks and paper ink works.<\/span><\/p>\n

What materials and techniques do you use?
\nI paint abstract works with acrylic, pastel, spray paint, marker and pencil. And to punctuate the flow of paint, I draw ink pieces influenced by images real or imagined\u2014dreams, nature and adventures meaningful to me<\/p>\n

What kind of inspirations go into your work?
\nHeavily inspired by nature around me, you can find me at the beach most days. I keep Moleskine’s full of notes I have written in nature, including poems, drawings, connections and observations. These notes become a direct gateway to the beginning of an artwork. <\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

In what space do you like to create most?
\nI like to create within a space which is filled with all my favourite things; tools, books, plants, music and sun. This space isn’t any sole place, I’m creating wherever feels like home.  .<\/p>\n

What has been your favourite or most important work to date?
\nIn September of this year, I started an initiative called \u2018Notes for Friends\u2019. <\/p>\n

I premised this project broadly, I wanted people to feel comfortable doing this for themselves or nominating their friends. All they knew was that they were getting free artwork with a poem on the back. But it was more than that, it was something to look forward to and cherish deeply.<\/p>\n

 Creating these works was fulfilling and creative, I produced over 45 original works \u2014 no two artworks the same because no two people are the same.<\/p>\n

 I was inundated with individuals speaking positively of this project, I was able to reconnect with people, I listened, and most importantly gave what I have to offer. Art, love and that warm feeling I’m searching for. Explore this project in full here: https:\/\/www.evelynmarina.com\/journal\/notes-for-friends<\/a><\/p>\n
