Introducing our 2022\u00a0Homegrown<\/em>\u00a0artists<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Late last year, in partnership with The Regional Arts Development Fund (RADF)<\/em>, we called-out to local artists on the Sunshine Coast.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We were seeking projects that fostered a sense of connection and belonging for Sunshine Coast residents, which had a positive impact on the community.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We would like to thank all the amazing artists that applied this year \u2014 we were blown away by the projects.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
