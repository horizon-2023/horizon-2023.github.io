Beneath The Surface<\/em> <\/em>is a new interview series that explores the less seen (and sometimes buried) side of creativity \u2014 the emotions, the ups and downs, the self-doubt, and, of course, the vulnerabilities.<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

It\u2019s 8.45 and we\u2019ve just arrived in Pomona. We\u2019ve brought Tim Birch along for the ride.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Amanda\u2019s home is tucked away from the quiet country town and we are warmly met by two cattle dogs, Reese and Skittle, at the front gate.  <\/p>\n\n\n\n

We exchange hugs, hellos, and high fives, and Amanda gives us a tour of the block. The home (a grand old Queenslander) is beautiful and everything you could imagine in a building from this era. There\u2019s a wrap-around porch, a rainforest behind it, and \u2014 what seems to be \u2014 a makeshift hospital room beneath the surface of the deck. It’s a fitting metaphor for the interview. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

And just like that: we get a glimpse into Amanda\u2019s life. It was time to dig a little deeper.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
